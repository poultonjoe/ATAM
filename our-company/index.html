Our company

AT Asset Management offers a highly personalised service, with tailored solutions to meet your requirements in banking, finance and investments. The financial services we offer are complemented by a range of premium services designed to support your passions, beliefs and ideas.

At AT Asset Management we go beyond investment, building strong client relationships and delivering consistent results. Clients work with our team to select investment vehicles covering the entire asset spectrum including cash, fixed income and equities, as well as a range of alternative offerings such as private equity and hedge funds. Besides our services in wealth management, we can offer you access to our partner networks in corporate and investment banking to meet your business goals.

Philosophy

AT Asset Management aims to build long and lasting relationships with clients that deliver the highest standards whilst providing specialist expertise in the field of portfolio management.

Driven by care, passion and the commitment to excellence, our goal is to be the trusted partner of our clients by offering dedicated and professional services.

We think and act as owners of our clients’ portfolios, to deliver sustainable long term value to our clients.

Our aim is to develop the best possible strategies in order to assist in servicing your needs. To fulfil our ambition of meeting your goals, we provide you with access to specialists spanning Asia and Europe. Our transparency is a key feature in our relationship and allows you relax, whilst we take care of the rest.

Principles

Our principles and core values define the way we think and act. They sharpen our focus on our clients and act as our compass - having a good compass enables us to get back to our fundamental purpose and beliefs, especially when we are faced with situations we have not come across before.

Our client-first approach is committed to upholding the highest levels of integrity. To ensure this we exercise prudence and sound judgement combined with a considered approach to managing risks as we seek to deliver sustainable investment returns.

We are aware that our actions today help shape your tomorrow. Because of this, we have an ongoing commitment to manage your assets respectfully, always taking a considered approach when it comes to your wealth.

We believe in long term relationships that are built upon high levels of performance and quality of service. When our clients do well, we do well.Andy LimManaging Director

Management team

Andy LimManaging Director

Andy is the chief investment officer of AT Total Return Fund and the co-founder of AT Asset Management. He has more than 12 years of wealth management experience with international banks where he manages high net worth individuals’ investment portfolios. Andy employs bottom-up and value-oriented research and analysis to produce above-average yields and total returns for the portfolio. His knowledge and experience in the financial industry has given him an edge when it comes to developing absolute return investment strategies with low portfolio volatility.

Teresa TayDirector

Teresa is a portfolio manager of AT Total Return Fund and the co-founder of AT Asset Management. She has 8 years of wealth management experience in the banking industry where she manages investment portfolios of high net worth individuals from Asia Pacific. Teresa sits on the investment committee to provide her expertise in effective portfolio construction and she oversees the ongoing daily operations within the company. She earned her double degree in BBus (Accounting) / BBus (Banking & Finance) from Monash University Australia with minors in Statistics and Economics.

Cheah Chee MunPortfolio Strategist

Cheah Chee Mun has 12 years of experience in the financial industry. He writes economic research and develops portfolio strategies. He is also responsible for performing investment portfolio analysis and monitoring portfolio risk. Prior to joining AT Asset Management, he assumed roles as Investment Advisor and Regional Risk Controller at commercial and investment banks, which include Standard Chartered, Deutsche and CIMB Bank. Cheah Chee Mun received a Bachelor of Science with Honors in Banking & Finance from the University of London. He also holds the Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) designation.

Joe PoultonMarketing

Michael KristensenIndependent Advisor
